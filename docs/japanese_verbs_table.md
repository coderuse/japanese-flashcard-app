## Notes:
- **Bold text** = Romaji
- **Kanji** = Chinese characters (when applicable)
- **Hiragana** = Japanese syllabic script
- All conjugations are in polite form (masu form)
- Verbs #4 (kuru) and #5 (suru) are irregular verbs
- Verbs ending in -ru are mostly ichidan (Group 2) verbs
- Verbs ending in other sounds are mostly godan (Group 1) verbs
